About OA Drinks & Snacks

Founded in Lagos in 2011, OA Drinks & Snacks set out to do one thing: bring honest, natural taste back to everyday refreshment. No artificial colours, no fake sweetness, no shortcuts — just real hibiscus, real tiger nuts, real ginger and spices sourced fresh from local markets. Over a decade later, we serve hundreds of homes and shops across Nigeria with the same care and quality that started it all.
